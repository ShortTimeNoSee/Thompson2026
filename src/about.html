Hi, I'm Nick.

And I'm running for governor because California deserves better.

I'm not your typical politician. I don't play games, make deals, or climb ladders. And that's exactly what California needs right now.

The Reality

Sacramento thinks it owns you. They regulate how you live, work, and even breathe. They treat your rights like privileges they can revoke whenever they want.

The Message

This campaign is a wake-up call. A reminder that power belongs to the people, not the bureaucrats who think they can run your life better than you can.

The Goal

To prove that Californians are done with the status quo. We're tired of being treated like subjects instead of citizens. It's time for real change.

What I Stand For

Individual Rights

Your rights aren't up for debate or subject to majority vote. They're yours, period. The state needs to back off and let you live your life.

Economic Freedom

Sacramento's regulations are suffocating small businesses while big corporations write the rules. Time to level the playing field and let people build their dreams.

State Overreach

The state has no business in your personal choices, your wallet, or your property. They work for us, not the other way around. Time to remind them of that.

Real Transparency

No more backroom deals. No more hidden agendas. Every Californian deserves to see exactly how their money is being wasted by Sacramento.

Let's Be Real

"The state doesn't own you. And it's time they remembered that."

Look, I get it. You're probably thinking: "Another politician making promises." But I'm not here to promise you anything except this: I'll be the brick wall between you and a government that can't stop trying to control your life.

The career politicians in Sacramento are experts at one thing: making your life more complicated while padding their own pockets. Every year they pass hundreds of new laws telling you what you can and can't do with your own life.

This campaign isn't about winning a cushy job in Sacramento. It's about sending a message: Californians are done being treated like subjects in their own state.

You don't need a politician to run your life. You need someone who'll tell Sacramento to back off and let you run it yourself.

Ready to Push Back?

This isn't just another campaign. It's a message to Sacramento that Californians are done being pushed around. Time to remind them who they work for.
