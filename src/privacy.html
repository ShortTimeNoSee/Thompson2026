Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

No lawyer-speak. No hidden clauses. Just honest disclosure.

Last updated: December 2025

This policy is open source and licensed under the Liberty-ShareAlike Public License (LSA-1.0). Feel free to adapt it for your own campaign or project.

The Short Version

  • We collect only what we need to run the campaign and communicate with supporters
  • We never sell your data. Not now. Not ever.
  • You can delete your data by emailing us
  • We use minimal third parties and they're listed below
  • No tracking cookies. We do not use analytics that follow you around the web.

What We Collect

Data Required? Why We Need It
County Yes To show geographic support across California
Name No Displayed publicly with your signature (defaults to "Citizen" if blank)
Comment No Displayed publicly with your signature
Email No Only if you opt into newsletter or volunteer list

Name No To personalize emails (if provided)

Data Required? Why We Need It

Comment Yes Displayed publicly after approval

When You Use the Contact Form

Data Required? Why We Need It
Email Yes To respond to your message
Name No So we know how to address you
Message Yes The content of your inquiry

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encrypted in transit: All data sent to/from our servers uses HTTPS/TLS
  • Cloudflare protection: Our site is protected by Cloudflare's security infrastructure
  • Minimal storage: We don't keep data longer than necessary
  • Access control: Only campaign leadership can access supporter data
  • No shared passwords: Each team member has individual credentials

Third Parties We Use

We believe in transparency. Here's every third party that may receive your data:

Service Purpose What They Receive
Cloudflare Hosting, security, Workers IP addresses, request data
Brevo Email delivery Email addresses, names (for subscribers)
GitHub Code hosting None of your personal data

We do NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or any tracking analytics
  • Facebook Pixel or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • You'll receive campaign updates, blog post notifications, and occasional calls to action
  • Every email includes an unsubscribe link that works immediately
  • We comply with CAN-SPAM and California email laws
  • We never share your email with other campaigns, organizations, or anyone else

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Correct: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email: nicholas4liberty [at] gmail.com

We'll respond within 30 days (usually much faster).

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies: To remember your preferences during a visit
  • localStorage: To save your name/email for convenience when commenting

We do NOT use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies that follow you across the web.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you're a parent and believe your child has submitted data to us, contact us and we'll delete it.

California-Specific Rights

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell, so this doesn't apply)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits you to request information about disclosure of personal information to third parties for direct marketing. We don't share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we'll:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Notify subscribers via email if the changes affect them
  • Keep previous versions available in our GitHub repository

Contact

Questions about this policy? Concerns about your data?

Email: nicholas4liberty [at] gmail.com

Or use our contact form

Our Philosophy

"The right to privacy is not about having something to hide. It's about having something to protect: your autonomy, your dignity, and your freedom to think and act without surveillance."

As a libertarian campaign, we believe your personal information is your property. We collect only what's necessary to communicate with supporters and run an effective campaign. We're transparent about what we collect because you deserve to know. We do not monetize your data because that would make us no better than the surveillance state we're running against.
