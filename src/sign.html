Why This Matters

Most campaigns pay professionals per signature to collect these. That creates a barrier where only wealthy candidates or those backed by big donors can get on the ballot.

We're crowdsourcing the bureaucracy.

You're not just a voter. You're an active agent in tearing down the barriers that keep independent candidates off the ballot.

Every petition you submit is worth ~$0.82 to the campaign. But more importantly, it's a statement that democracy shouldn't have a paywall! We're all in this together.

Questions?

Do I need to be a registered voter to circulate?

Yes, you must be a registered California voter to sign. But you can circulate your own signature, so if you can sign, you can circulate.

Can I get signatures from people in other counties?

All signatures on one petition form must be from the same county. But you can print multiple forms for different counties if you know people spread across California. Just note you'll have to drop off each form separately in each county!

What if I mess up the form?

Just print another one! The PDF is free to download and print as many times as you need.

Do I really need to print it double-sided?

YES. This is a requirement. Single-sided printing will result in rejection. Follow the manual flip instructions if you don't have a duplex printer.

Can I mail it instead of dropping it off?

Not right now. We're still confirming mailing protocols with county registrars. For now, please drop it off in person to ensure it's properly received.
