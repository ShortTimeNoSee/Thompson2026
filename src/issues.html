What I'm Fighting For

These aren't just policy positions. They're the battles I'm willing to fight to restore your freedom.

Sacramento has forgotten that they work for you. Time to remind them.

Issues

Individual Rights

Your Rights Aren't Up for Debate

Your rights don't come from government. They don't get to vote on whether you deserve them. They're yours, period. Time to remind Sacramento who they work for.

Economic Freedom

Stop Punishing Success

Sacramento's regulations are strangling small businesses while big corporations write the rules. Cut the red tape. Stop treating success like a crime. Let people build their dreams.

Government Overreach

They Don't Own You

The state has no business in your personal choices, your wallet, or your property. They work for us, not the other way around. Time to put them back in their place.

Your Money

Stop Funding Their Failures

Sacramento treats your wallet like their personal ATM. Every failed program, every bloated bureaucracy, every pet project - funded by your hard work. Keep what you earn.

Real Transparency

No More Backroom Deals

No more hidden agendas. No more deals cut behind closed doors. You deserve to see exactly how Sacramento wastes your money and who they're really working for.

Housing Crisis

Get Out of the Way

Sacramento created this housing crisis with their regulations and red tape. Stop blocking development. Stop making it impossible to build. Let people create homes.

Self-Defense Rights

Defend Yourself

You have the right to protect yourself and your family. Sacramento's gun laws only disarm law-abiding citizens while criminals ignore them anyway. Restore your rights.

Property Rights

Your Land, Your Rules

Your property is yours. Not the state's. Not the county's. Yours. Stop letting government steal private property for their projects. Defend property rights.

Education Choice

Parents Know Best

Parents should choose their children's education, not bureaucrats. School choice, homeschool freedom, educational options. Get government out of the classroom.

Healthcare Freedom

Your Health, Your Choice

Healthcare decisions belong between you and your doctor. Not you, your doctor, and a bureaucrat. Cut regulations strangling healthcare innovation and choice.

Justice Reform

Equal Justice Under Law

The law should apply equally to everyone. No special treatment for the connected. No different rules for the powerful. Justice means justice for all.

Environmental Solutions

Innovation, Not Regulation

Protect the environment through innovation and incentives, not crushing regulations that kill jobs while doing nothing for the planet. Smart solutions, not government control.

Ready to Fight Back?

These battles won't be won by politicians making promises. They'll be won by people who are tired of being pushed around and ready to push back.
