About Nicholas A. Thompson

Nicholas A. Thompson is a passionate advocate for liberty, individual rights, and transparent governance. Born and raised in California, Nicholas has dedicated his life to fighting for the freedoms that allow all Californians to thrive.

Nicholas understands the challenges facing Californians today. His campaign for Governor in 2026 is rooted in the belief that government should serve the people by protecting their rights, not by limiting their potential.

As Governor, Nicholas aims to reduce unnecessary regulations, lower taxes, and ensure that every Californian has the opportunity to succeed based on their own merits. His vision for California is one where innovation flourishes, communities are empowered, and the government respects the personal freedoms of all individuals.

Nicholas's leadership is defined by a commitment to transparency, accountability, and a deep respect for the principles of liberty that have made California a beacon of opportunity.
