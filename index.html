About Me

I'm Nicholas A. Thompson, a 2026 candidate for California Governor. My campaign stands for liberty, transparency, and accountability. I’m committed to empowering Californians to make their own choices and succeed without unnecessary government interference.

Key Issues

Healthcare Reform

Reducing costs through a freer market and eliminating unnecessary regulations.

Economic Liberty

Promoting a business-friendly environment by reducing taxes and bureaucratic hurdles.

Education

Empowering parents and students with more choices in education.

Environmental Stewardship

Encouraging innovative solutions for sustainable growth.

My Vision for California

My vision is to make California a beacon of liberty and innovation. I want to foster a state where individuals can pursue their dreams without undue government interference, where businesses can flourish, and where our environment is protected through responsible, market-driven solutions.
