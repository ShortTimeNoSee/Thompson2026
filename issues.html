Key Issues

Healthcare Reform

Eliminating unnecessary regulations to lower healthcare costs and improve access by allowing a freer market to thrive.

Economic Liberty

Reducing taxes and government barriers that hinder business growth, ensuring that Californians have the freedom to innovate and succeed.

Education

Empowering parents and students with more educational choices, encouraging competition to improve quality and lower costs.

Promoting innovation in sustainable practices by reducing government interference and allowing the market to lead in environmental solutions.
